ax
/æks/  
noun · 名词

释义

1.a tool used for cutting down trees and splitting wood, consisting of a heavy metal blade attached to a long handle
He swung the ax and split the log in two.
2.砍伐树木或劈开木材的工具，由装在长柄上的重金属刀片组成
他挥动斧头，把木头劈成了两半。
